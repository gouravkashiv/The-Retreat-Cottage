About Us

Welcome to The Retreat Cottage, a luxurious 5 bedroom independent villa nestled in the heart of the beautiful pine valley. With breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains, this villa is the perfect getaway for families looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and immerse themselves in the tranquility of nature. The spacious and well-appointed bedrooms offer plenty of room for everyone to relax and unwind, while the open-plan living and dining area provides the perfect space for spending quality time together. Enjoy the peace and quiet of the mountains while you breathe in the fresh pine scented air. Whether you're looking to relax, rejuvenate or just spend time with loved ones, The Beatrix Manali Cottage is the perfect place to do it all. Book your stay now and experience the best of what nature has to offer.
